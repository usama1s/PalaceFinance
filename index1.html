What is Palace Finance?

The Palace Finance serves as the backbone of a rapidly growing ecosystem aimed towards bringing liquidity and new use cases to the Arbitrum network. The protocol's underlying mechanism dynamically adjusts $PUSD's supply, pushing its price up or down relative to the price of $ETH.

Why choose us?

Unlike previous algorithmic tokens, $PUSD is not pegged to a stable coin it is instead pegged to $ETH.

Why is this?

Palace Finance believes in the potential of Ether, and has chosen to align its mission to both provide value to and derive value from $ETH's future growth. $PUSD aims to become the main medium of exchange on Arbitrum: this will be achieved by providing a mirrored, liquid asset to $ETH. One of the primary shortcomings of past algorithmic tokens has been a lack of use cases, leaving no good reason for somebody to want to use or hold them. In order to successfully maintain the peg in the long-run, the Palace team will maintain a focus on innovation around enhanced functionality and use cases.

Tokenomics

  • Initial Supply : 200 (100% will go for linitial liquidity)
  • Sell fee - 6%
  • liquidity adjustment

The 6% sell fee helps prevent a price dump of $PUSD. A liquidity adjustment is used to increase the price when it moves below the peg price. When the price goes down to the peg price, the core team activates a liquidity adjustment and the token price increases by 5% each day.

What is Lucky Key?

The Lucky Key (LKEY) is an NFT token (ERC721) and performs similar operations to the old tomb protocol's share tokens, but the LKEY can be used for many other purposes as well which makes it so great than other tomb protocol share tokens.

Stake to Genesis Pools

Users can stake their LKEYs into the USDC or USDT genesis pools and get a 15% bonus of the NFT price, from the total staked amount(A balance increase of 20% of Total NFT price staked). Staked LKEYs will be locked for 15 days.

Stake to Boardroom

Users can stake LKEY to the boardroom and be rewarded with PUSD.

Exchange with Palace NFT

Users can later exchange LKEYs with Palace NFTs at a 1:1 exchange rate. Palace NFTs are placed in the NFT Marketplace and used as NFT characters in the Palace gamefi.
